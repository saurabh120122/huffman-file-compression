**Generated Huffman Codes:**  
a: 0

**Encoded String:**  
0100110001000111
**Decoded String:**  
abacabad

Explanation:
Original text length: 8 characters × 8 bits = 64 bits
After Huffman Encoding: 17 bits
Compression Ratio ≈ (1 - 17/64) × 100% = 73%

Size before compression: 64 bits
